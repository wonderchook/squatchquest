#### Organic IG Growth Playbook (draft outline)

**Philosophy:** Build a real community, not a follower count. Every interaction should feel like it came from someone who actually cares about PNW outdoor culture, small-town tourism, and weird Bigfoot stuff.

**Target communities:**
- PNW hiking / outdoor recreation
- Small town tourism / chambers of commerce
- Scavenger hunt / geocaching enthusiasts
- Bigfoot / cryptid culture (unironic AND ironic)
- Agritourism / farm trail
- Local WA/OR small business

**Daily actions:**
- Like 20–30 posts from target community hashtags
- Leave 5–10 genuine comments (not "Great post! 🔥" — actual thoughts)
- Follow 5–10 accounts that align with our community
- Follow back anyone who follows us and fits the community
- Reply to every comment on our posts within 4 hours
- Share relevant stories from accounts we follow (with credit)

**Content mix:**
- 40% Behind-the-scenes (event setup, QR printing, scouting locations)
- 25% Bigfoot/cryptid culture (fun, shareable, meme-adjacent)
- 20% Small town spotlights (businesses we work with, towns we love)
- 15% Event promotion / results

**🚨 Human flag protocol:** When engagement surfaces a response that sounds like a real person interested in booking or partnership, flag immediately to Kate + Marika in #squatchsighting with context.
