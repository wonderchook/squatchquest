👁️ What is SquatchSighting?

We build custom Bigfoot-themed QR code scavenger hunts that bring people together in real places. Whether it's a downtown shopping district, a local trail system, or a community event — we create fun, phone-powered adventures that get people exploring.

Each hunt features:

  • Weatherproof QR code stickers placed at real locations
  • A mobile-friendly web app — no downloads required
  • Custom Bigfoot-themed trivia and challenges at each stop
  • Progress tracking so participants can see how many sightings they've made
  • Support for local businesses and community organizations

🗺️ How It Works

We work with you to design a scavenger hunt tailored to your community:

  • Pick your locations — local businesses, trail markers, landmarks, or event stations
  • We build the hunt — custom questions, Bigfoot lore, and a beautiful mobile app
  • QR stickers go up — weatherproof stickers that participants scan at each stop
  • People explore! — walk, discover, answer questions, track progress

Everything runs in the browser. No app store. No accounts. Just scan and go.
