How It Works

  1. Find a QR code at a participating business
  2. Scan it with your phone's camera — no app download required
  3. Answer the trivia question — Bigfoot-themed, naturally
  4. Visit all the stops and track your progress
  5. Complete the hunt to enter the prize drawing!

What is SquatchSighting?

We create custom QR code scavenger hunts that bring people to local businesses. Each hunt is themed, fun, and designed to get folks exploring their downtown on foot.

  • Browser-based — works on any phone, no downloads
  • Custom trivia and challenges at every stop
  • Progress tracking so you know how close you are to finishing
  • Prize drawings for participants who complete the hunt
  • Built to support local businesses and community events

Who's Behind This
